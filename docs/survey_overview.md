# Survey Instrument Overview

## Project

**Current Employment Survey Design**

This project contains a Qualtrics web survey instrument designed to measure household composition, employment status, job holding, hours worked, earnings, education, and demographic characteristics.

## Purpose

The instrument was designed as a survey methodology project to practice translating labor-force and household measurement concepts into a structured web-survey questionnaire. The project emphasizes respondent flow, person-level measurement, household roster logic, and structured data collection.

## Instrument Features

- Consent/introduction screen
- Household roster and household-size questions
- Person-level demographic questions
- Employment status measures
- Work-for-pay/profit items
- Temporary absence from work
- Multiple-job questions
- Hours worked and usual hours
- Earnings and household income measures
- Education and background measures
- Qualtrics survey flow suitable for web administration

## Relevance

This project demonstrates experience with web-survey design, questionnaire programming, structured survey flow, and employment/labor-force measurement. It is relevant to roles involving survey materials review, print/web/telephone survey design, survey data quality, and survey operations.
